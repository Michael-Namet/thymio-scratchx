Getting Started

1. Download Aseba Http Bridge

Connect your Thymio robot to the personal computer (either with the wireless dongle or with the USB cable) and turn on the Thymio robot by pressing the central button

3. Load the Thymio ScratchX Extension on ScratchX

Launch the extension by going to the following URL:

https://github.com/aseba-community/thymio-scratchx/blob/gh-pages/thymio_extension.js

NOTE:

4. Wait for the green light

When you see the indicator light in the "More Blocks" tab turn green your ready to start using the extension!

See the section on the homepage for Supported Languages, for information on alternate block translations.

Now take a look at the ScratchX Blocks and the learning activities to learn how to use the extension.
